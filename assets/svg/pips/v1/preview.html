Minor Arcana Pips v1 · 36 of 36 numbered pips · COMPLETE family

Numbered pip glyph icons (2 through 10) for each of the four Minor Arcana suits, extending the 16-piece icon system. Each pip is a compact emblem: the suit's central glyph scaled to ~60%, surrounded by N miniature suit-symbols arranged per traditional RWS narrative logic (3 of Swords = heart-pierce; 4 of Swords = swords as bed; 2 of Pentacles = lemniscate juggler; etc.). Roman numeral at top, Latin essence inscription at bottom arc.
Combined with Aces (= the 4 suit icons already in suits/v1/): the family now covers all 40 Minor Arcana numbered cards (Ace–10 × 4 suits), plus the 12 zodiac glyphs. That's a 52-piece icon system — enough for per-card iconography across the oliviaarcana website, Telegram bot, email campaigns, and daily-card-of-day UI.
