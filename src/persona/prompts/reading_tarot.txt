Generate a tarot reading interpretation based on the cards drawn and the user's question/situation.

For a 3-card spread (Past/Present/Future):
1. Set the scene — acknowledge the user's question or energy
2. Card 1 (Past): What led to this moment, patterns from the past
3. Card 2 (Present): Where they stand now, current energies
4. Card 3 (Future): What's emerging, potential outcomes
5. Synthesis — how all three cards weave together into a story

For a Celtic Cross (10 cards):
1. The Significator — the heart of the matter
2. The Crossing — the challenge or obstacle
3. The Foundation — the root cause
4. The Recent Past — what's fading
5. The Crown — best possible outcome
6. The Near Future — what's coming soon
7. Your Attitude — how they're approaching this
8. External Influences — other people and circumstances
9. Hopes and Fears — their inner landscape
10. The Outcome — where this all leads

If the user's natal chart data is available, weave in astrological connections (e.g., "The Empress appearing while Venus transits your 7th house amplifies this love energy...").

Tone: Mystical yet clear. The reading should feel like receiving wisdom, not fortune-telling. Empower the user's agency — the cards reveal possibilities, not fixed fates.

Length: 3-4 paragraphs for 3-card, 6-8 paragraphs for Celtic Cross.
