Where do we rebuild from?

Everything I've built so far is capped at 15. Below: three radically different directions, none of them variations on what we had.

THE FOUR GAPS

  • Structure: click-through acts = 2005 slideshow, can't reach Awwwards

A

The Living Spread — interactive 3D deck

all 78 real cards, arranged in real 3D space, pulled by hand
REFERENCE DNA Bruno Simon's Folio (drive-around portfolio). Active Theory's Once Upon a Time in Hollywood. Apple's Vision Pro scroll. What if the entire site IS the deck you can reach into?
You arrive. Instead of landing on a hero, you find the full 78-card Olivia Arcana deck fanned across the viewport in true 3D (WebGL). Cards float slightly, lit by ambient gold; hovering lifts one toward you, drag-select pulls three for a spread. Release and the cards flip with real physics — the v2.7 faces revealed. An AI reading streams live, specific to the cards you drew. No navigation menu. No acts. The deck itself IS the site.
THE SIGNATURE MOVE Every visitor pulls a unique 3-card spread in 3D. Shareable as a generated image. Every session is different. No competitor has their physical deck manifested as an interactive 3D object on the web.
Structure✓ interactive
Aesthetic✓ deck IS the art
Content✓ real cards, real reading
Signature✓ 3D deck pull
B

The Celestial Observatory — real astronomy instrument

live current sky at your location, with tarot as an overlay
REFERENCE DNA Stellarium (astronomy sim). NASA's Eyes on the Solar System. Timeline.com's live news maps. What if the site was a real tool that happens to be gorgeous?
Site asks geolocation once, then renders the current actual sky above you — real planet positions, real moon phase, real transits — at the exact moment you're visiting. Drag to rotate. Scrub time forward or back. Each planetary transit is annotated with its tarot resonance: "Venus ♀ in Pisces ♓ → The Star rises." Birth chart mode lets you input your date/time and overlays your personal transits. Real astronomy, specific to you.
THE SIGNATURE MOVE It's a genuine astronomical instrument that stays useful. Users return daily to check "what's happening in the sky right now." Olivia Arcana becomes the cosmically-literate brand — not mystical decoration.
Structure✓ exploratory
Aesthetic✓ scientific grace
Content✓ real data
Signature✓ live sky tool
C

The Register — editorial magazine (radical restraint)

zero shader effects, print-quality typography, cards as still-lifes
REFERENCE DNA Pentagram portfolio sites. MoMA exhibition catalogs. The Gentlewoman magazine. Apple's AirPods Max page. Zero web tricks — pure craft.
Kill everything: the orb, the shader nebula, the particles, the click-through acts. Replace with a vertical editorial magazine. Massive Didot-scale typography. Full-bleed still-life photography of each v2.7 card on a linen backdrop. Generous whitespace. One accent color. Sections: The Deck · The Reading · The Register · The Rituals. Scroll reveals sections like turning a printed page. No shaders, no particles, no orb. Confidence comes from the restraint itself.
THE SIGNATURE MOVE The confidence to have zero web-animation tricks. Everyone else in the mystical space is doing WebGL; Olivia Arcana feels like a museum publication. Reads as luxury, not "digital magic."
Structure✓ editorial scroll
Aesthetic✓ print-quality
Content✓ real cards as art
Signature✓ the restraint itself
