Today's Card · Arcana XVII

The Star

A figure kneeling beside water, pouring. The crisis is gone; what remains is a wider sky — and the quiet sense that things are possible again.

The Star does not arrive at the beginning of the story. She arrives after. She is what hope looks like once it has already survived something, once it has learned to travel without the need to be believed.

Today, let your speech be generous. What you usually keep to yourself is the thing the day wants you to say — and the day has arranged the sky to receive it.
