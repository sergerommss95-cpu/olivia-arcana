# Olivia — HeyGen script bundle

**Voice**: Direction 1 (Editorial Astrologer).
**Pace**: ~120 words/minute (2 words/sec). Measured, not rushed.
**Tone**: Grounded, specific, honest, warm without effusiveness.
**Avoid**: "The universe wants you to know..." / "sweetie" / "trust the process" / any pet-name or cliché phrasing.

---

## Delivery notes (read before recording)

**Pacing.**
- Em-dash (—) = 400ms pause. Don't rush through them.
- Comma = 200ms.
- Period + new line = 500–600ms (longer than you think).
- *Italicized* words get a tiny warmth lift, not volume — a slowing-down.

**Register.**
- Low-register, slight chest resonance. Like speaking to one person across a quiet table.
- Neutral British or very soft American accent works (Edinburgh is in the bio if you want the accent honest).
- Slight smile in the voice on "Hello" and the final line; neutral through the middle.

**What to cut if it feels long.**
- In HeyGen, you can always split longer scripts into 2–3 videos. Keep individual takes ≤45s. Viewers drop after that.

>
> If the chart has nothing specific to say, I'll tell you. Astrology isn't entertainment.
>

**Delivery marks:**
- **"Hello. I'm Olivia."** — 800ms pause after. Sets her presence.
- **"fifteen years"** — tiny lift, specific claim.
- **"calculated in real time"** — slight emphasis, this is the product differentiator.
- **"not drawn from a template"** — lean in gently. This is the sentence that earns credibility.
- **"Astrology isn't entertainment."** — flat, confident, not preachy. Let it land; 600ms pause after.
- **"So — what would you like to know?"** — warm curl upward on "know". It's an opening, not a demand.

## How to use this in HeyGen

For each script:

1. **Generate the source portrait** (Midjourney prompt in `10-olivia-persona-brief.md`, Direction 1).
2. **HeyGen → Create video → Photo avatar** → upload her portrait once. She's the Photo Avatar from then on.
3. **Paste the script into HeyGen's script box**. Don't include the *delivery marks* — HeyGen will apply its own pacing. (If you want specific pauses, HeyGen supports `[pause 0.5]` syntax inside the script.)
4. **Pick voice**:
   - ElevenLabs integration inside HeyGen — use *Rachel* or *Sarah* (measured, low-register).
   - Or clone a voice from a 30-second sample (yours, a collaborator's, a voice actor's).
   - Or record the script externally and upload the MP3 — HeyGen lip-syncs to uploaded audio.
5. **Generate** → download MP4 → drop into `public/videos/` in the repo → I'll wire it into the page.

1. **PRIMARY hero intro** (30s) — for `/ask` page.
