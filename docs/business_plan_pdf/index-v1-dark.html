Part Three · Market
A $5.69B market growing 20% per year.
The astrology-app market tripled between 2020 and 2026 and shows no sign of plateauing — cultural mainstreaming, smartphone saturation, and AI personalization all compound the demand.

Demand signals

  • 43% of women aged 18-49 in the US identify as astrology-curious or believers (Pew Research).
  • Usage spikes 650% during major astrological events — Mercury retrograde, eclipses, solar returns.
  • Personalized natal-chart tools increased engagement by 30-45% over generic horoscopes.
  • Paid compatibility readings drove 15-25% spending uplift per user.

Underserved markets

English-only incumbents ignore ~60% of the addressable base. Ukraine, CIS, MENA (Arabic) and LATAM (Portuguese, Spanish) have no category-defining native app — most competitors offer only machine-translated EN content.

Olivia's 8-language rollout captures what Co-Star, Chani and Nebula structurally cannot — the non-English 60% of the global astrology audience.
