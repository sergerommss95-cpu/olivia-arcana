VI · Recommendations

Five pricing moves.

VI

1. Hold, then raise.

VIP Monthly at $6.50 is 46% below market median. Hold for acquisition through launch year. A/B test $7.99 vs $8.99 in Q3 on new cohorts only — grandfather existing subscribers.

2. Deck as a three-SKU ladder.

Standard $49 (retail headline, 42% margin) · Collector $89 (52% margin) · VIP-member $35 (subscription funnel). All three include free worldwide shipping as the marketing anchor. Every deck sold to a non-member becomes an upsell path into $65/yr.

3. Source live-expert supply from UA / RU astrologer communities.

At $30-40 per 24-hour video reading, freelance Ukrainian- and Russian-speaking astrologers earn strong income; supply is abundant on Facebook groups and Telegram channels. Scale English supply via Upwork / Fiverr partnerships.

4. Implement Stripe Adaptive Pricing (PPP) in Q2.

Purchasing-power parity raises prices +20-30% in DE / CH / FR / UK and discounts -30-40% in LatAm, MENA non-Gulf and Ukraine-domestic. Captures revenue left on the table in Tier-1 without pricing out Tier-3.

5. Add a $9.99 / 25-question Ask-the-Stars pack.

Current packs ($0.99 / $1.99 / $4.99) clip at the micro-txn ceiling. A $9.99 pack unlocks a mid-elasticity buyer cohort and raises ARPU on this revenue line by ~40%.
