## PILLAR 1: Text Slideshows (The Volume Engine)

### Why This Works
Astroscope generates 70M+ views/month with NOTHING but text slides. No voice, no face, no video editing. Just zodiac personality content formatted as swipeable carousels.

### Format Specification
- **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 (9:16 TikTok slideshow/carousel)
- **Slides per post:** 5-8 slides
- **No audio required** (TikTok adds trending sound automatically to slideshows)
- **Production time:** ~5 minutes per post using templates

### Slide Structure
```
SLIDE 1 (HOOK):
  Bold title that targets a sign or group
  "Things Scorpio does that make no sense"
  or "Your biggest red flag based on zodiac sign"

SLIDES 2-6 (CONTENT):
  One sign per slide (or one trait/fact per slide)
  Sign glyph + name + 2-3 lines of punchy text
  Example: "♏ SCORPIO
  Will ghost you for 3 days then act like
  nothing happened. And somehow YOU
  feel like the crazy one."

SLIDE 7 (CTA):
  "Follow @oliviaarcana for daily readings"
  or "Which sign are YOU? Comment below"
  Logo + link reference
```

### Visual Template (Celestial Noir)
```
Background: #0D0D1A (void black) with subtle gradient to #1A1A3E
Text: White (#F5F0E8) primary, Gold (#D4AF37) for sign names
Font: Bold sans-serif (Inter Bold or similar), 42-60px
Glyph: Zodiac symbol in element color, large
Layout: Centered, generous spacing, easy to read on phone
Border: Optional 1px gold line frame
Branding: Small "OLIVIA ARCANA" at bottom of each slide
```

## PILLAR 2: Pick-a-Card Readings (The Engagement Engine)

### Why This Works
- **Highest watch time** — viewer must watch through to see "their" pile
- **Highest comment rate** — "Drop your pile number" is frictionless engagement
- **Highest share rate** — people tag friends "pick your pile!"
- **The Barnum Effect** — vague-but-specific readings feel deeply personal
- **Algorithm-as-fate** — viewers believe the video "found them" for a reason

### Format Specification
- **Duration:** 60-90 seconds
- **Piles:** 3 (optimal for TikTok length)
- **Audio:** AI voiceover (ElevenLabs "Olivia" voice) + ambient mystical music
- **Visuals:** Animated card flip sequences + text overlays
- **Captions:** Mandatory (word-by-word, synced)

### Video Structure
```
[0-5s] HOOK + SELECTION
  Text: "Pick a card — this message was meant to find you"
  Visual: 3 face-down cards (or 3 crystals/colors) labeled 1, 2, 3
  Voice: "Spirit has a message for you today. Choose your pile."
  Pause beat to let viewer pick

[5-10s] "You've chosen? Let's begin. Pile 1..."

[10-30s] PILE 1 READING
  Card flip animation → reveal card
  Voice: 20-second interpretation
  Key phrases as text overlays
  Theme: specific but universally relatable

[30-50s] PILE 2 READING
  Same structure

[50-70s] PILE 3 READING
  Same structure

[70-80s] CLOSING + CTA
  "Drop your pile number in the comments"
  "Share if this resonated with someone you know"
  "Follow for your daily reading"
```

### Reading Content Guidelines (The Barnum Sweet Spot)

Readings must be **specific enough to feel personal, vague enough to apply broadly:**

**GOOD (feels personal):**
"Someone from your past has been on your mind lately. Not because you miss them — but because you're finally understanding why that chapter had to close."

**BAD (too vague):**
"Good things are coming your way."

**BAD (too specific):**
"Your ex named Mike will text you on Thursday."

**Formula per pile:**
1. Acknowledge a current emotional state (validation)
2. Reference a specific situation (creates recognition)
3. Deliver insight or prediction (value)
4. End with empowerment (leaves viewer feeling good)

### Theme Rotation
- **Monday:** Love/Relationships ("What your person is thinking")
- **Wednesday:** Career/Money ("A financial message for you")
- **Friday:** Weekend energy ("What's coming this weekend")
- **Sunday:** Week ahead ("Your week in 3 piles")

### Visual Design
```
Background: Dark gradient (#0D0D1A → #1A1A3E)
Cards: Custom AI-generated tarot card art
  Style: Celestial noir — dark with gold foil accents
  Back: Gold geometric pattern on void black
  Front: Rich illustration with sign/element imagery
Card flip: Smooth 3D rotation animation (0.8s)
Pile labels: Gold text, numbered 1-2-3
Ambient particles: Subtle floating gold dust
Music: Ethereal, calm, slightly mysterious
Captions: White bold, word-by-word reveal, center-bottom
```

### Automation Pipeline
```
1. Claude generates 3 pile readings (60-80 words each)
2. ElevenLabs voices all 3 readings + intro/outro
3. Remotion/FFmpeg renders:
   - 3 card-back → card-flip → card-face animations
   - Voice synced to each pile reveal
   - Ambient music bed
   - Burned-in captions
4. Export as 1080x1920 MP4 (60-90 seconds)
```

## WHAT NOT TO DO (Common Mistakes)

1. **Don't post generic horoscopes** — "Today is a good day for Aries" gets zero views
2. **Don't over-produce** — fancy editing doesn't beat consistent, emotionally resonant content
3. **Don't post without captions** — 80%+ of TikTok is watched on mute
4. **Don't ignore the first 3 seconds** — if the hook fails, nothing else matters
5. **Don't be inconsistent** — the algorithm rewards daily posting above all else
6. **Don't copy other creators' exact content** — develop Olivia's unique voice
7. **Don't neglect comments** — reply to every comment in the first hour
8. **Don't spread too thin** — master 2-3 formats before adding more
9. **Don't use generic zodiac memes** — they feel lazy and damage the premium brand
10. **Don't make scary predictions** — empowering > fear-mongering (long-term trust)
